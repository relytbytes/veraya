# Tech Moat — Deck Slide

*Drop-in slide copy. One headline, three bullets, one closer. Plain-English.*

## Slide title

**Veraya learns each restaurant — and that lead compounds.**

*(Alt titles: "The intelligence is built in, not bolted on." · "A data moat that widens with every shift.")*

---

## Three bullets

- **A self-learning brain, not a dashboard.** Vera forecasts the day, watches how it actually goes, and re-weights itself overnight — so accuracy improves at *each venue*, automatically, with no analyst.

- **Predicts and prescribes.** Real-time P&L projection, demand forecasting, prep quantities, and reorder timing — Veraya tells the operator what's about to happen *and what to do about it*, before it happens.

- **Intelligence end-to-end.** Snap a supplier invoice and costs update themselves; the floor advances itself through each meal; regulars are recognized before they sit. The AI runs the busywork.

---

## Closer line

**The moat isn't a feature — it's the months of per-venue learning inside every live deployment. A competitor can clone a screen; they can't clone the data.**
