## Visual identity
- **Logo:** the circle-V mark (`/veraya-icon.png`) — teal/gold circuit-V in a
  ring. Use the bare circle, not an app-tile (rounded-square) treatment.
- **Wordmark:** "Veraya" in gold (#E7B23E) on dark; extrabold, tight tracking.
- **Palette:** teal `#21A090` (primary) + gold `#E0A82E` (accent), dark
  `#0C1A1E` surfaces.
